>Tilemap collider utility for 2DDL Pro

How use:
>Add TileMapCollisionGenerator script to any GameObject
>Add your tilemap to tilemap field
>Set output collisions GameObject

>Automatic generation:
1.Set chackbox "Generate on Awake"
2.Colliders will be generated before game started

>Non-Automatic generation:
1.Press "Generate collisions" button and collisions will be generated
